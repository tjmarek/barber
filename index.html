Meet Cynthia Rivera

Founded and operated by Cynthia Rivera, The Gentlemen's Quarters blends classic barbering traditions with a clean, modern aesthetic. Every detail is curated to create a calm, focused space where the only agenda is your best look.

With over two decades of experience, Cynthia specializes in men's precision haircuts, skin fades, beard design, and detailed shaping — combining head shape, hair growth patterns, and personal style to craft a cut that grows out clean.
