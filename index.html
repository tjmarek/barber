About

The Gentlemen's Quarters Studio

Founded and operated by master barber Cynthia Rivera, The Gentlemen’s Quarters blends classic barbering traditions with a clean, modern aesthetic. Every detail—from the lighting to the music—is curated to create a calm, focused space where the only agenda is your best look.

With over two decades of experience, Cynthia specializes in men’s precision haircuts, skin fades, beard design, and detailed shaping and lining. Her approach is equal parts technical and artistic, combining head shape, hair growth patterns, and personal style to craft a cut that grows out clean.

Whether you’re maintaining a timeless look or refining something new, she’ll walk you through options, maintenance, and styling so you know exactly how to wear it between visits.

The studio operates on an appointment-only basis to respect your time and ensure every service gets the attention it deserves. No walk-ins, no double-booking—just you, the chair, and a meticulous barber focused on the details.

From quick clean-ups to full transformations, The Gentlemen’s Quarters is where your cut, fade, and beard game are brought up to standard—and kept there.
